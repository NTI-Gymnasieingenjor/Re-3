About us

Ordering food with ReCubed works like with any other takeaway platform, but without the guilt. ReCubed is developing a fully green, circular delivery service that includes environmentally friendly pickup and delivery as well as the use of ReCubed's durable, sturdy, and reusable food containers.

Our team has a diverse background in food safety, business development and design, and extensive experience of what destruction the misuse and exploitation of our natural resources can lead to. We are determined to put an end to the amount of single use materials consumed in Sweden’s restaurants. Together, let’s make a choice that we can be proud of and that leaves a better world for all of us. Join us and make Sweden a pioneer in the sustainable takeout industry.
