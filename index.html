About us

Ordering food with ReCubed works like with any other takeaway platform, but without the guilt. ReCubed is developing a fully green, circular delivery service that includes environmentally friendly pickup and delivery as well as the use of ReCubed's durable, sturdy, and reusable food containers.

How we work

1

Pick your favourite restaurant and order for delivery or pickup.

2

Your food is delivered to your doorstep with our ReCubed Box which is reusable.

3

Return the ReCubed Boxes to any partnered restaurants or return your boxes to our delivery person on your next order.

ReCubed gives you a choice

The high amount of takeaway food consumed every year naturally entails a high production of disposable packaging.

Through our platform, services, and containers, restaurants and customers can make ordering takeaway guilt-free, green, and sustainable.
